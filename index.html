Grab Some Popcorn, Watch a Movie, and Leave a Review!

Lets cut the crap and admit the truth. Movie critics can be harsh and overrated. They expect to much and never give fair reveiews. This is where we come in as a community! With this website you can search up and movie and provide a proper review of what you thought, so that others can see what their peers think instead of relying on movie critics (BOOOOOO!). Sure rotten tomatoes and other ratings may get it right sometimes, but every movie is being advertised as "the best movie of the century/decade" and I just don't trust them anymore. I would rather see what normal everyday people think about the movies instead of trusting the critics. So watch a movie and then come back and leave your thoughts about it on our website! NO SPOILERS please!

Not Feeling a movie? Choose a TV Show!

Not everyone has time to sit down and watch hours of something, or maybe you do. Either way a good TV show that keeps you guessing on whats going to happen next may be the perfect thing for you. Well look no further because you are in the right place! Whether you want a blast from the past or something more recent you can look for it all on our website. Sit down and binge watch your favorite TV show and leave the review here, so that others can do the same. Maybe the TV show wasn't for you, either way leave the reveiw in order to save other people the time of starting something they won't enjoy either! Once again NO SPOILERS please!

Gamers Unite!

Whether you enjoy PC or console gaming we use one of the largest gaming API's/Database to allow you to search, reveiew, and find other games for your liking. Help others find games for them by leaving meaningful reveiews as well as finding more games for you to enjoy! Enter a game title and we will use the API to return various games that are similar to it that you might want to buy. Before buying be sure to read other peoples reveiws of the game! Just like the movies every company out there rates games either better or worse than they really are. Theres been a drought of good games being released, so leave a great reveiw about a game that either encourages or discourages someone from buying the game!
